## Purpose

---

# Project Overview

This case study applies the Data Analytics Project Methodology (DAPM) to a warehouse operations and inventory management scenario.

The objective is to analyse operational data, identify business problems, evaluate data quality, and generate evidence-based insights that support inventory management and operational decision-making.

The project follows a structured analytical workflow, progressing from business understanding through data validation, preparation, exploratory analysis, business insights, and executive reporting.
