# Engagement Summary

The engagement began after Apex Warehouse & Distribution Pvt. Ltd. requested assistance in understanding the operational challenges affecting its warehouse and inventory management processes.

The client reported recurring business issues, including inventory shortages, excess stock, delayed order fulfillment, inventory discrepancies, and increasing operational complexity as the organization continued to grow.

Rather than implementing a new operational system, the objective of this engagement is to analyze the client's existing business processes and available operational data to identify inefficiencies, support evidence-based decision making, and recommend opportunities for operational improvement.

The project follows the Data Analytics Project Methodology (DAPM Framework), progressing through structured analytical phases beginning with business understanding and continuing toward business insight generation and executive reporting.

# Current Progress

The project is currently progressing through the DAPM Framework.

Subsequent phases will focus on discovering available datasets, validating data quality, preparing analytical datasets, performing exploratory analysis, generating business insights, and producing evidence-based recommendations.
